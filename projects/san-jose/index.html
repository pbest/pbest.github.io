Project Background

San Jose is one of the nation's ten largest cities and the home of Silicon Valley. With a diverse & unique culture, city hall wants to ensure their digital services are inclusive of all residents.

The mayor's office wanted to align its goal of 'tech leadership' with the needs of city residents.
This project began with a mission but no clear objectives. We were brought on to first help the city understand what they should focus on, then build a solution.
We started with a week of immersive learning.

[Video of kickoff]
With a better sense of how the city works and what leadership wanted us to explore and avoid, we dove into research
[ Example of research ]
Analytics, Comparative, Diary Study, Resident Interviews
This allowed us to define a high-level concept, and create a rubric for success
[ Resident Asssistant (defined) ]
[ 5 point rubric ]
We defined 5 different use cases for a 'resident assistant'* (*we were constrained to using existing content)
[ 5 concepts ]
Then we did a preference test to understand how residents felt about the concepts
