Project Background

When refugees arrive in the US, they have 90 days to acquire the knowledge and skills necessary to adjust to life in the U.S. — a process known as cultural orientation. After this time they must fully support themselves.

Our Solution
Refugees receive inconsistent support throughought the process. The learning materials provided were not approachable, conveneint, or easy to access.
Choose a chapter Settle In is designed to keep you informed, engaged, and motivated throughout the resettlement process. Revisit lessons at any time, or skip around until you find the lesson that's most relevant to your needs.
Test and track your knowledge with quizzes and badges. With short videos and interactive lessons, you can learn about a wide range of pre- and post-arrival resettlement topics like traveling to the U.S., housing, and looking for a job. Settle In will track your learning achievements without storing any personally identifiable information.
